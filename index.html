Who We Are

Endless Strands is a Toronto-based nonprofit empowering individuals experiencing hair loss due to cancer or alopecia. We organize hair donation drives and partner with organizations that create and distribute wigs free of charge to those in need. We are partnered with A Child's Voice Foundation and a proud member of Imagine Canada.

Why donate your hair?
  • A real-hair wig helps someone feel like themselves again.
  • High-quality wigs can cost thousands of dollars. Your donation helps provide them at no cost.
  • Donating your hair is a tangible way to show support to someone in need.
