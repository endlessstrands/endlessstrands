Who We Are

Endless Strands is a Toronto-based nonprofit empowering individuals experiencing hair loss due to illness or medical treatment. We coordinate hair donation drives and partner with organizaitions that create and distribute wigs free of charge to those in need.

Why donate your hair?

Hair loss can be an overwhelming experience, especially when it's caused by medical conditions like cancer or alopecia. It can affect a person's self-esteem and sense of identity. By donating your hair, you are:

  • Restoring confidence for people facing illness
  • Saving families the high cost of quality wigs
  • Spreading kindness in a personal and tangible way
Who can donate?

Anyone with hair that meets specific guidelines can donate:

  • Minimum length: 30 cm (12 in), layered hair accepted if longest layer meets the length
  • Acceptable: virgin hair, grey hair, hair cut and stored in braid/ponytail
  • Not acceptable: dyed or bleached hair, permed hair, dreadlocks, hair swept off the floor

Our Story


Jessica has been donating her hair since Grade 6, after learning about hair donation on the news. Inspired by how such a simple act could make a big difference, she founded Endless Strands to raise awareness and show others just how easy and meaningful hair donation can be.
