Donate your hair

Hair donation is a simple way to support people experiencing hair loss due to medical conditions like cancer or alopecia. At Endless Strands, we work to make the process easy and accessible to everyone.

By visiting one of our Toronto-based drives or donate on your own through another organization, your contribution helps someone feel like themselves again.

Donate at an Endless Strands drive

We host regular walk-in hair donation drives in Toronto throughout the year. Our events are open to the public, and no appointment is required! Please ensure that your hair meets the guidelines for donation.

At our drives, we:

  • Cut your hair for you on-site
  • Offer snacks and handmade thank-you gifts for donors

Donate directly to another organization

If you can't attend a drive, you can still make a difference by donating directly to one of these reputable Canadian organizations. They accept hair by mail and provide guidelines for preparation on their websites.

  • Angel Hair for Kids*
  • Continental Hair*
  • Chai Lifeline Canada

Donation guidelines

To make sure your hair can be used at an Endless Strands drive, please follow these guidelines:

  • Hair must be at least 4 inches (10.2 cm), but we  strongly  prefer hair that is at least 12 inches (30.5 cm)
  • Hair must be clean and dry (washed within 24 hours)
  • Grey hair is accepted
  • Dyed or treated hair is acceptable, although the manufacturer determines the viability of all donations upon receipt
  • Dreadlocks, wigs, or synthetic extensions are not accepted
