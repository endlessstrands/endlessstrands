Endless Strands Privacy Policy Effective Date: September 21, 2025 Endless Strands (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our organization, whether by visiting our website, attending our events, making a donation, or otherwise engaging with us. By engaging with Endless Strands, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect We collect the following types of information: a) Personal Information When you interact with us, we may collect: Contact information (e.g., name, email address, phone number, mailing address) Donation information (e.g., donation amount, payment method, transaction date) Event participation information (e.g., registration details, consent for photos or videos) Hair donation details (e.g., length, condition, and preferences for acknowledgment) b) Non-Personal Information We may also collect data that does not directly identify you, such as: Website usage data (pages visited, time spent, browser type) Aggregated statistics about donations and participation 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: Process donations and issue tax receipts (if applicable) Communicate updates about our events, programs, and impact Respond to inquiries and requests Recognize and thank donors and volunteers (with consent) Improve our services, events, and website Comply with legal and regulatory requirements 3. How We Share Your Information We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances: Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who help us process donations, host events, or manage communications. Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process. With Your Consent: If you explicitly authorize us to share your information (e.g., publicly recognizing your donation). 4. Data Security We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. This includes using secure payment processing for online donations and limiting access to personal data to authorized personnel only. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. By interacting with us, you acknowledge that we cannot guarantee absolute security. 5. Your Choices You have the right to: Access or correct your personal information Opt out of receiving our communications (using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us) Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and accounting requirements 6. Children’s Privacy Our programs are open to people of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. 7. Data Retention We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective Date” at the top indicates when it was last revised. Updates will be posted on our website. 9.
