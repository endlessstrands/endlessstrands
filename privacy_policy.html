Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 21, 2025

Endless Strands (“Endless Strands,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects and is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, volunteers, event participants, and website visitors (“you” or “your”). This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and safeguarding of personal information in accordance with applicable privacy legislation, including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

By providing personal information to Endless Strands, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • “Personal Information” means any information about an identifiable individual, as defined under PIPEDA, including but not limited to name, email address, telephone number, and donation or payment details.
  • “Non-Personal Information” refers to data that does not identify you and cannot reasonably be linked to you.

2. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information by lawful and fair means and only for purposes that are reasonable and necessary. This information may be collected through our website, donation forms, event registration processes, volunteer sign-ups, email communications, and other interactions with Endless Strands.

The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Identification information: name, email address, phone number
  • Financial information: donation amount, payment method, transaction details (processed through secure third-party payment processors)
  • Participation details: event registrations, consent forms, photographs or media (with prior consent)
  • Hair donation details: information voluntarily provided regarding hair length, condition, and related preferences

We may also collect certain non-personal information automatically through our website, including IP address, browser type, and aggregated analytics data, for the purpose of improving our online services.

3. Use of Personal Information

Endless Strands collects, uses, and retains personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and acknowledge donations
  • To communicate with donors, volunteers, and participants regarding events, programs, and organizational updates
  • To administer our operations, including record-keeping, accounting, and compliance with legal obligations
  • To recognize donors and supporters (with express consent)
  • To improve our programs, events, and digital platforms through feedback and analytics
We will not use personal information for purposes other than those for which it was collected, except with consent or as required or permitted by law.

4. Use of Cookies and Google Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze website traffic, and understand how visitors interact with our content.

a) Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. These may include:

  • Essential cookies – required for the proper functioning of our website
  • Performance/analytics cookies – used to collect information about website traffic and usage patterns
You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, doing so may affect the functionality of the website.

b) Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect non-personal information such as:

  • IP addresses (anonymized where possible)
  • Device type, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Geographic region (approximate)
Google Analytics uses cookies to generate aggregate reports about website traffic. This information helps us understand user behavior and improve our website and programs.

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, visit https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information

Endless Strands does not sell, trade, or rent personal information. We may disclose personal information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: To third-party vendors or service providers engaged to perform services on our behalf (e.g., payment processing, email communications, event registration platforms), who are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal and Regulatory Requirements: Where disclosure is required or permitted by law, regulation, or court order.
  • With Consent: Where you have expressly authorized disclosure (e.g., public recognition of a donation).

6. Safeguards

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards designed to protect personal information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

While we take reasonable measures to protect personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. You acknowledge that we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Retention and Destruction

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. When personal information is no longer required, it will be securely destroyed, erased, or anonymized in accordance with our data retention policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information held by Endless Strands
  • Request corrections or updates to your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for the use of your personal information, subject to legal or contractual restrictions
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to retention obligations under applicable law
Requests may be made in writing to the contact information provided below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Endless Strands does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. Where applicable, parental or guardian consent will be obtained before collecting or using information from minors.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Endless Strands reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or organizational practices. Updates will be posted on our website, and the “Effective Date” will be revised accordingly.

11. Contact Information

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, to exercise your rights, or to make a privacy-related complaint, please contact us at contact@endlessstrands.org.

Complaints will be investigated promptly, and corrective measures will be taken where justified.
