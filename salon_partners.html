Salon Partners

When you book a haircut at one of our partnered salons and mention that you're donating through Endless Strands, you'll receive a $50 reimbursement towards the cost of your haircut. It's our way of saying thank you for your generosity.

If are interested in donating your hair through a partnered salon, please click here.

If you own or manage a salon and would like to become a partner, send us an email at contact@endlessstrands.org to express your interest.
